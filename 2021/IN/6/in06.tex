\section{Question IN 02}
Let $ u(t) $ denote the unit step function . The bilateral laplace transform function  $ f(t) = e^t u(-t) $ is 
\begin{enumerate}
\item[A] $ \frac{1}{s-1}$ with real part of $ s> 1$
\item[B]$\frac{-1}{s-1}$ with real part of $ s> 1$
\item[C]$\frac{1}{s-1}$ with real part of $ s< 1$
\item[D]$\frac{-1}{s-1}$ with real part of $ s< 1$
\end{enumerate}
\section{Solution}
\fi
Here we need bilateral laplace transform of $e^t u(-t)$\\
Laplace transform of $e^{-t} u(t)$ is :
\begin{align}
= \frac{1}{s+1} \quad \sigma > -1
\end{align}
Time reversal property:
\begin{align}
e^t u(t) \rightarrow \frac{1}{-s+1} \quad \sigma < 1 \\
e^t u(-t) \leftrightarrow \frac{-1}{s-1} \quad Re(s) < 1
\end{align}
