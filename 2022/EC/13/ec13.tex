According to Mason's gain formula , transfer function can be given as :
\begin{align}
\text{TF } &= \frac{\sum_{k-1}^{n} P_k \Delta_k}{\Delta} = \frac{P_1\Delta_1 + P_2\Delta_2}{\Delta} \\
&= \frac{G_1 + G_2 }{1 + G_1 H}
\end{align}
